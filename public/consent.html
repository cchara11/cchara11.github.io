Welcome to the Audio-driven speech animation for virtual characters crowdsourcing questionnaire. The purpose of the questionnaire is to evaluate different speech animation approaches within a variety of audio scenarios. In each scenario, your main task involves selecting your one video between two similar ones based on your own preference. More detailed instructions will be referred in the next pages.

The questionnaire takes about 25-30 minutes to complete. (Part 1 requires more time because you need to get familiar with the selection) The participation is completely anonymous and you are certify that you are at least 18 years old.

By clicking Agree, you confirm that you have read the terms and conditions and you agree to participate in the experiment
