Angels VS Demons

Angels VS Demons is a 2-player game that was developed in Unity game engine, during the 2016 Game Jame in Amsterdam, Netherlands. Each player controls one of the available teams (angels/demons) each one with different objectives. Each game round consists of a specific ritual that can be accomplished with a specific potion pattern. The "Demons" team objective is to collect those potions whereas the "Angels" team tries to prevent them doing so.

Supermarket Sim

Supermarket Sim is a serious game, which was developed during the Games And Agents master course of Utrecht University. The buyers's behavior during a supermarket visit was studied, and simulated in this serious game. The game was developed as a way to help marketing trainees or supermarket managers, in order to to study different ways of product placement among the supermarket shelves as a way to maximize their profits. The simulator was developed in Unity game engine.

Augmented Reality using Oculus Rift and Depth Sensor Dissertation Paper

My dissertation paper that was written during my Bachelor Thesis project at University of Cyprus. The paper addressess a way of augmenting virtual objects to a real-time video feed viewed directly from an Oculus Rift Virtual Reality head-mounted display. This is known as video See-Through display. The current paper is written in Greek.
